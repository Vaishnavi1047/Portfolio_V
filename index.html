Sentimental Analysis

Sentiment analysis, a branch of Natural Language Processing (NLP), focuses on understanding and extracting sentiments or opinions from text data. It involves analyzing text to determine the sentiment expressed, which could be positive, negative, or neutral. Sentiment analysis has numerous applications across various industries, including marketing, customer service, social media monitoring, and product development.

Sustain Mart

SustainMart, an e-commerce platform for buying and selling eco-friendly products, promoting sustainability, and educating consumers on environmental impact. I developed a scalable backend with Node.js and Express, managed data with MongoDB, and designed a multi-language chatbot for sustainability queries. Additionally, I enabled real-time shipment tracking with Socket.io and created a user-friendly interface using Tailwind CSS and JavaScript.
